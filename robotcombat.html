Bissan Bersa

30-Pound Shuffling Shell Spinner

Key Design Decisions

Shell Weapon Type: A shell offers many benefits over more traditional vertical and horizontal spinners with some unique design considerations that cause most builders to shy away from them. A shell was chosen for this robot because it allows for the weapon to protect all sides of the robot simultaneously. Since we do not have the facilities to practice driving it will improve our effectiveness if driving skill is not a key aspect of our strategy. A shell also has the potential for large energy transfer due to the increased weight of the spinning weapon compared to traditional designs as well as acting as a stabilizer in the case of being launched in the air by another robot.

Shuffler Drive System: Most combat robot competitions allow a weight bonuses for "non-traditional" locomotion.
