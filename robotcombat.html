30-Pound Shuffling Shell Spinner

Key Design Decisions

Shell Weapon Type: A shell spinner was chosen mainly for its balance of offense and defense. It allows for 360-degree weapon coverage which protects the robot from any angle, while the comparatively large mass delivers strong kinetic energy transfer. Additionally the spinning shell provides gyroscopic stabilization, reducing the risk of flipping when taking hits from other robots.

Shuffler Drive System: The V1 design utilized two wheel drive with skids for stabilization, but during competition we encountered substantial issues with floor inconsistencies impacting the skid performance. For V2 we solved this issue by replacing the wheels with shuffler drive modules. This removes the need for skids as each shuffler module has at least two points of contact with the ground and there is an added benefit of a 1.5x weight bonus for "non-traditional locomotion".
